Overview

NPDE25 will take place June 17–20, 2025, on the King's Buildings Campus of the University of Edinburgh. It will consist of a series of lectures delivered by experts in the fields of high-dimensional PDEs, scientific machine learning, PDE-constrained optimization, and data assimilation.

Lunch will be provided each day of the summer school. A session of poster presentations will take place on Wednesday, and short research talks will be organized for Thursday afternoon.

Venue

Lecture Location: Joseph Black Building, Theatre 100.

Lunch & Coffee Break Location: James Clerk Maxwell Building, Magnet Cafe.

Registration will begin at 10:15 a.m. on Tuesday, June 17, just outside Lecture Theatre LT100 in the Joseph Black Building.

Conference Dinner

The Conference Dinner will be held at The Rabbit Hole. You can view its location HERE.
