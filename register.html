Travel and childcare support

Limited funding is available to cover accommodation and travel expenses. To qualify, you must be a PhD student, affiliated with an institution in the UK or the EU, and be willing to give a talk or present a poster at the summer school. To apply for travel support, please indicate your interest during registration and send your CV and a short motivation statement including an overview of your travel costs to npde25.summerschool@ed.ac.uk.

A portion of our funding is dedicated to covering childcare costs incurred while attending the summer school. Please indicate during the registration process whether you'd like to apply for support.
