Overview

NPDE25 will take place June 17–20, 2025, in the James Clerk Maxwell Building of the University of Edinburgh (see Practical Information). The mornings will be devoted to introductory lectures, while more in-depth topics will be presented in the afternoons. Lunch is provided on every day of the summer school. Depending on participants' availability, a session of poster presentations and short research talks will be organised for Thursday afternoon.
